Egg Nog Pancakes

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups flour • 3 1/2 tsp. baking powder • 1 tsp. salt • 1 Tbs. sugar • 1 1/2 cups egg nog • 1 egg • 3 Tbs. melted butter • 2 oz. package chopped nuts (optional) • Cooking oil

Directions

In a large bowl, sift together dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, salt, sugar). Make a well in the center and pour in the egg nog, egg and melted butter. The egg nog will make thicker pancakes, so if you want them thinner, you can add more or replace some of the egg nog with milk. Mix chopped nuts into the batter or reserve them to put on top of cooked pancakes later. Heat a lightly-oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter, per pancake, onto the griddle. Flip when you see little bubbles forming on the top. Brown on both sides and serve hot. Use a basting brush to add melted butter while still hot. Add your favorite syrup.
