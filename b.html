Fruit and Nut Syrup

Ingredients

1 cup blueberries, chopped strawberries or other fruit or berries • 4 Tbs. maple syrup • 2 Tbs. chopped nuts

Directions

Mix ingredients in a small sauce pan. Cover and simmer on low heat, stirring occasionally for about 10 minutes. If using frozen fruit, you may need to heat a little longer.
